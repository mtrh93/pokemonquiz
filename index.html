How To Play

  • Choose your Type on the selection to play the quiz.
  • You'll be shown a question, and four possible answers.
  • Click the answer you believe is correct.
  • If it's correct, your score beneath will update.
  • If it's incorrect, your score beneath will not update.
  • If you get stuck press the home button to restart.
